Our Mission

We believe everyone deserves a clear path to their fitness goals. FitPath provides curated, community-driven training paths that show you exactly what to do, in what order, and why — without the noise.

What We Offer

24+ Training Paths
Strength, endurance, flexibility, and sport-specific paths — from beginner to advanced
Progress Tracking
Mark workouts and milestones as complete and track your training across every path
Custom Paths
Build your own training plan tailored to your goals and share it with others
Community-Driven
Paths are built and improved by real athletes. No corporate gatekeeping.
Open Source
FitPath is open source. Contributions from coaches and athletes worldwide are welcome.
Free Forever
All core features are completely free. No paywalls, no premium tiers.

Built by Athletes, For Athletes

FitPath was created by people tired of vague fitness advice and overcomplicated programs. We believe in straightforward, practical guidance — what to do, how to progress, and when to push harder.

Every path is community-maintained. Every suggestion is considered. Every contributor is valued.

Want to Get Involved?

FitPath is open source and welcomes contributions. You can:

  • Create or improve training paths
  • Suggest new fitness paths
  • Report bugs or request features
  • Spread the word

Check out our GitHub repository to get started.
