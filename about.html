Our Mission

We believe every developer deserves access to structured learning resources. FitPath.dev provides curated, community-driven roadmaps that show the essential skills and tools you need to grow in your career — without the fluff.

What We Offer

24+ Roadmaps
Role-based paths (Frontend, Backend, DevOps) and skill-based guides (React, Node.js, Python, and more)
Progress Tracking
Mark topics as complete and track your learning journey across every roadmap
Custom Roadmaps
Create your own learning paths tailored to your goals and share them with your team
Community-Driven
Roadmaps are built and updated by the community. No corporate gatekeeping.
Open Source
Our project is open source and contributions from developers worldwide are welcome
Free Forever
All core features are completely free. No paywalls, no premium tiers

Built by Developers, For Developers

FitPath.dev was created by developers who were tired of vague learning resources and corporate training programs. We believe in straightforward, practical guidance without fluff or marketing speak.

Every roadmap is community-maintained. Every suggestion is considered. Every contributor is valued.

Want to Get Involved?

FitPath.dev is open source and welcomes contributions. You can:

  • Create or improve roadmaps
  • Suggest new learning paths
  • Report bugs or request features
  • Help translate content
  • Spread the word

Check out our GitHub repository to get started.
