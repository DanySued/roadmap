Privacy Policy

Last updated: April 2024

1. Introduction

FitPath.dev ("we", "us", "our" or "Company") operates the FitPath.dev website. This page informs you of our policies regarding the collection, use, and disclosure of personal data when you use our service and the choices you have associated with that data.

2. Information Collection and Use

We collect several different types of information for various purposes to provide and improve our service to you.

  • Email Address: When you create an account or contact us
  • Device Information: Device type, browser type, IP address
  • Usage Data: Pages visited, time spent, roadmaps created
  • Cookies: For authentication and preferences (stored locally)

3. Local Storage

FitPath.dev uses browser localStorage to save your preferences, progress, and custom roadmaps. This data is stored locally on your device and is never sent to our servers without your explicit consent.

4. Data Security

The security of your data is important to us but remember that no method of transmission over the Internet is 100% secure. We use commercially reasonable means to protect your personal data, but we cannot guarantee its absolute security.

5. Third-Party Services

We may link to third-party websites including GitHub, YouTube, Discord, and Twitter. These websites have their own privacy policies and we are not responsible for their content or practices.

6. Children's Privacy

Our service does not address anyone under the age of 13. We do not knowingly collect personally identifiable information from children under 13. If you are a parent or guardian and you are aware that your child has provided us with personal data, please contact us immediately.

7. Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update our Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of any changes by posting the new Privacy Policy on this page and updating the "Last updated" date at the top.

8. Contact Us

If you have any questions about this Privacy Policy, please contact us at privacy@FitPath.dev
